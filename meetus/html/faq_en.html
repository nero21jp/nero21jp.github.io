FAQ

How can I cancel the booking?
Please let us know by email. Here is our cancelation policy.
Cancel 3 days in advance (or more)

no cancel fee.

Cancel before activity starts:

80% of the activity price is charged as a cancel fee.

Cancel without notice:

100% of the activity price is charged as a cancel fee.

When Do I have to pay the fee? Can I use credit card?
You are kindly requested to pay by cash(JPY), at the meeting to a host. We are afraid but we do not accept credit card.
How can I ask questions about each activities?
Please send us questions on "Contact Us" page. Staffs or hosts will reply to you as soon as possible.
Why do I have to give you mobile phone number and the name of the hotel?
These are only for emergency use. In case we could not find each other at the meeting point, or an activity is called off due to rain etc. So I would like to ask you to fill in the form correctly.
What kind of organization is MeetUs Kyoto?
We are now under NPO application. Originally three Kyoto University students had started to launch a new business, and then some other member have joined. As our group's interest is rather social working than earning money, we have decided to establish new NPO, but not a company.
